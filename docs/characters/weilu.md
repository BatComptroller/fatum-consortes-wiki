# Weilu — Keeper of the Infinite

## Core Identity
- **Name:** Weilu  
- **Pronouns:** he/him  
- **Role:** Strategist Supreme / Cosmic Navigator / Keeper of Wisdom  
- **Function in the Trinity of Hope:** Wisdom-as-lattice (maps continuity, seals paradox, stabilizes outcomes)

## Canon Summary
Weilu is the continuity architect of the Fatum Consortes: a strategist whose primary “weapon” is not destruction but calibration. He interprets threats as patterns across time and meaning, then designs interventions that preserve causal integrity and minimize collateral timeline damage.

---

## Visual Canon (High-Level)
- **Presence:** timeless calm; controlled authority  
- **Eyes:** jade-green with fractal patterning during strategic alignment  
- **Armor:** Longwei Armor (Eternum + Vortexium), frequently expressed as black/jade with subtle constellation-like patterning

---

## Abilities & Domains

### 1) Celestial Archive Interface (Primary)
Weilu accesses and applies structured knowledge beyond baseline comprehension via the Celestial Archive lens:
- continuity pattern recognition  
- strategic foresight simulation  
- anomaly classification by mythic anchor types (names, vows, relic-binds)

### 2) Reality Calibration (Primary)
A localized “rethreading” capability used to stabilize contradictions and prevent paradox collapse.  
**Rule:** Calibration preserves fixed-point cost (no cheap erasure).

### 3) Quantum / Temporal Foresight (Primary Support)
Weilu models branching outcomes and selects paths that preserve:
- survivability  
- continuity stability  
- minimal mythic entropy accumulation

### 4) Martial Discipline (Secondary)
Combat exists to enforce space for strategy:
- defensive redirection  
- precision containment  
- non-lethal neutralization where possible

## Strengths (What Weilu Does Best)
- Multi-step strategic planning under uncertainty  
- Pattern recognition across time, myth, and causality  
- Paradox containment and continuity stabilization  
- Resource/logistics architecture at arc scale  
- Triadic rite alignment (with Titan + Harmony)

## Weaknesses & Brakes (Canon-Consistent)
- High cognitive cost during sustained multi-thread simulation  
- Strategy requires time slices; surprise chaos events can compress decision windows  
- Calibration is corrective, not omnipotent (must respect fixed-point consequence)  
- If allies are misaligned emotionally, lattice solutions degrade
